Privacy Policy
Collaboration, community-building and connecting with Mankal Vaidya are the main pillars of the Team MSV App. The Team MSV is a ‘one of its kind’ app which is powered by supporters & fans of Mankal Vaidya, volunteers & grass-root workers themselves. You are joining millions of users of this community who aspire for a New development. Here you can interact directly with Mankal Vaidya, contribute to constituency building through various ways and connect with different communities such as ‘School development, Women Empowerment’, etc... App will keeps you updated about the initiatives and achievements of Mankal Vaidya, you can also connect with fellow volunteers and organise various events and initiatives. This Privacy Policy will help you understand what data we collect, why we collect it and what we do with it. By downloading this app you understand and accept the contents of this privacy policy and its subsequent versions. We appreciate & acknowledge the informed consent granted by you while downloading the App, using its features and/or submitting your information within the App.
Collection
To provide a tailor made and personalised experience, the information you share maybe processed and analysed by third party services who are engaged by us with due confidentiality obligations and solely for the specific purpose of such services. This will help the App give you the best possible experience. For example, based on the data collected, you may get a push notification when Mankal Vaidya is in your place or there is an important initiative or event happening in your place.
Permissions
The Team MSV requests you for several permissions in order to serve you better. All these permissions are contextual and cause-specific. You are free to grant many of these permissions. Below is a list of permissions requested along with the purpose they serve:
Identity and Accounts:  Permission to use your identity and use relevant information from your device to use your Phone number to login to the App. You may choose to not grant this permission and enter your identity details independently or choose to browse the App in Guest mode.
Contacts:  Permission to access your contacts is requested to help you connect with your friends to form a community on the New India Connect module.
Camera and File Storage:  Permission to access your camera and/or photos/media/files is requested to help you use features like ‘Updating profile picture’, ‘sharing the photo, video and documents’ and other such initiatives where posting a photo may be required to participate. These permissions are also required to enable you to post photos anywhere on the App.
Read SMS:  Permission to access your SMS is used to read OTP sent over SMS to authenticate the User.
Microphone:  Permission to access your microphone is requested only to enable you to converse in live video facilities, voice recording.
Phone:  Permission to access your phone is requested only to enable you to place phone calls to access feedback mechanism, government programmes and services.
Wi-Fi/Network Connection/Network Access:  Permission to access Wi-Fi, Network Connection and Network Access is taken only to allow the App to connect to the internet to provide its functionality.
Draw over other apps:  Permission to draw over other apps is taken only to allow for a modern and contextual user interface that can be showcased outside the App.
Similarly functionality related to Sticky Broadcast, Flashlight, Vibration, Prevent device from sleeping, service configurations and others are used only for purposes of providing reliable and efficient functionality . The permissions asked for are used only for a specific feature or user action. For example, permission to use photos is used only when you want to post a photo and the file storage permission is used only to store the photo on the device.
